# Interview 03 – Convenience-First Whole Foods Shopper

## Raw Notes
- Always goes to Whole Foods because it’s closest + trusted quality
- Rarely switches main store
- Generally doesn’t feel overpaid
- Exception: Vital Farms eggs → quality didn’t match price
- Biggest friction: geographic location / convenience
- Doesn’t check prices beforehand
- Does not visit multiple stores in one trip
- Goal: eat healthier food
