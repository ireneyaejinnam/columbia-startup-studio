# Interview Guide – RadiusCart

## Research Question
How do price-sensitive urban shoppers manage the trade-off between price and effort when grocery shopping?

## Key Questions
- Tell me about your last grocery run.
- What’s the most annoying part of deciding where to shop?
- Do you compare prices before leaving home?
- Have you visited two stores in one trip? Why?
- Tell me about a time you felt you overpaid.
- If you could change one thing about grocery shopping, what would it be?
