Key Findings

  • Winning version: V6 is the final copy for submission. Best resonance and intent with ICP; dealbreakers down to 2%.
  • What resonated: “You don’t need more clothes. You need outfits that actually go together”; concrete examples (e.g. 2pm meeting / 7pm drinks / 42°); visible $1.99; “What it looks like” sample output.
  • Remaining gap: Conversion confidence remains 0% — personas understand the offer and many would try it, but don’t yet believe it will work for their closet/context.
  • Recommendation: Ship V6 for landing and early traffic; iterate next on proof it works (e.g. before/after, week-1 results) to improve conversion confidence.
