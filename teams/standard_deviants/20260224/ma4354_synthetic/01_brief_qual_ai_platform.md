# Synthetic Test Brief: Qualitative AI Platform

## 1. What we're testing
Landing page copy for a new AI-powered qualitative research platform that automates transcription analysis, theme extraction, and cross-role synthesis for researchers in regulated and non-regulated industries.

## 2. Who's seeing this
- **Channel:** LinkedIn Ads, Google Search ("qualitative research software", "interview analysis tool"), Cold Outreach.
- **Context:** Professionals and academics looking for ways to speed up their research workflow without compromising data security or depth of insight.

## 3. Persona Buckets

| Bucket Name | Description | Typical Quote | Prevalence |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|------------|
| `compliance_hawk` | CFO or Data Lead in finance/healthcare. Obsessed with PII/PHI security and SOC2. | "If this data touches a public LLM without our VPC, it's a non-starter." | 25% |
| `sprint_consultant` | Qualitative consultant at a boutique agency. High volume of interviews, tight deadlines. | "I need to turn 20 hours of transcripts into a board-ready deck by Monday." | 30% |
| `striving_academic` | PhD student or early-career researcher. Transitioning from manual coding to mixed methods. | "I'm struggling to manage versions of my codes and my R scripts are breaking." | 25% |
| `skeptical_vet` | Senior researcher who believes "the only way to know the data is to read it 10 times." | "Can an AI really catch the subtext and emotional nuance of a frustrated user?" | 20% |

## 4. Product Description
A secure, workflow-aware AI platform that ingests interview transcripts and automatically identifies pain points, themes, and cross-role differences. It provides persistent context for longitudinal studies and ensures compliance for sensitive data.

## 5. Pricing
- **Individual:** $49/mo (Unlimited transcriptions, basic analysis)
- **Teams:** $199/mo (Collaborative workspace, custom codebooks)
- **Enterprise:** Custom (VPC deployment, SOC2, advanced security)

## 6. Copy Skeleton
- **Hero:** Value prop + Primary pain point solution.
- **Problem:** The manual "transcription tax" and analysis paralysis.
- **Solution:** AI-driven synthesis that understands your specific context.
- **Security:** "Compliant-first" architecture for regulated data.
- **Features:** Cross-role comparison, persistent memory, version control.
- **CTA:** Book a demo / Start free trial.

## 7. Evaluation Criteria
Using the standard evaluation schema:
- **Resonance:** Does it feel like it was built for them?
- **Clarity:** Do they understand *how* it works?
- **Intent:** Would they sign up for a demo?
- **Price Perception:** Does the value justify the cost?
- **Qualitative Feedback:** Specific likes/dislikes.

## 8. Stopping Criteria
- Minimum 5 rounds.
- >50% conversion "Agree+" across all personas.
- Qualitative convergence.
