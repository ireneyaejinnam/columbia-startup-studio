Executive Summary

V2 of the SynthetiQ landing page was tested against 60 synthetic personas. The copy moved away from hyperbole around speed and towards concrete features for security, auditability, and data integration. Resonance jumped to 100% and conversion confidence rose by 31pp overall.

Conversion Funnel

100% resonated with the copy (agree+). Of those, 80% expressed intent to try it, and 73% felt confident they'd convert. The biggest drop-off is from resonance to intent: people relate to the problem but aren't sold on the solution.

Researcher's Stance

The shift from speed ('20 minutes') to rigor ('Methodological Audit Trail') was the critical pivot. By addressing the skepticism of high-stakes researchers and compliance leads directly, we transformed the product from 'snake oil' to a 'defensible infrastructure.' The addition of R/JSON exports and CRM sync closed the loop on the primary workflow blockers identified in the user interviews.

  • Death of the 'Snake Oil' Label: Removing the '20 minutes' claim instantly improved credibility with Compliance Hawks (+53pp conversion confidence) and Skeptical Vets (+27pp).
